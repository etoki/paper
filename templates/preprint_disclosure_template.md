# Preprint Disclosure — boilerplate per venue

The Research Square preprint (DOI 10.21203/rs.3.rs-9513298/v1, deposited
2026-04-27) is the "parent work" of every conference sub-paper in this
portfolio. Each venue must be told about it. Use the venue-specific block
below verbatim in the cover letter / first footnote / acknowledgments
section, whichever the CFP requires.

If a CFP explicitly forbids prior posting (rare, but check), do **not**
silently submit — escalate to the author and pick a different venue.

---

## Common preamble (paste once at the top of the cover letter)

> A systematic review and meta-analysis of the Big Five personality traits
> and academic achievement in online learning environments was deposited as
> a preprint on Research Square on 2026-04-27 (DOI:
> [10.21203/rs.3.rs-9513298/v1](https://doi.org/10.21203/rs.3.rs-9513298/v1)).
> The present manuscript is a focused sub-analysis of that work and reports
> analyses **not** included in the preprint, as detailed in the next paragraph.

## Per-venue block

### ECEL 2026 (ACI)

> **Disclosure of related work.** A broader systematic review and
> meta-analysis by the same author was deposited on Research Square
> (DOI 10.21203/rs.3.rs-9513298/v1, 2026-04-27). The present paper extends
> that work with a *modality-stratified* random-effects meta-regression and
> a modality x trait interaction model, neither of which appears in the
> preprint. The ECEL submission therefore makes a distinct empirical
> contribution centred on the synchronous / asynchronous / blended axis
> requested by the conference's e-Learning scope.

### IEEE TALE 2026

> **Disclosure of prior posting.** The systematic review and meta-analysis
> on which this manuscript builds was deposited on Research Square as a
> preprint (DOI 10.21203/rs.3.rs-9513298/v1, 2026-04-27). The present TALE
> submission reports a study-level machine-learning pipeline (Logistic
> Regression / Random Forest / XGBoost) trained on the extracted features
> of the engineering / STEM subset, together with SHAP-based interpretation
> and a fairlearn-based fairness audit. None of these analyses appears in
> the preprint, which only reports the trait-level random-effects pool.

### ICEEL 2026

> **Disclosure of related preprint.** The underlying systematic review and
> meta-analysis is publicly available on Research Square (DOI
> 10.21203/rs.3.rs-9513298/v1, 2026-04-27). The present ICEEL paper extends
> that work with a Hofstede cultural-dimensions moderator analysis on the
> East-Asian subset and a focused narrative synthesis of the Japanese
> online-learning context. These cultural-moderator analyses are not
> reported in the preprint.

### ICERI 2026

> **Disclosure of preprint.** A more comprehensive systematic review and
> meta-analysis by the same author appears on Research Square as a preprint
> (DOI 10.21203/rs.3.rs-9513298/v1, 2026-04-27). The present ICERI submission

> meta-analytic interaction model that is not reported in the preprint.

## Cover-letter checklist

Before sending any conference cover letter, confirm:

- [ ] The preprint DOI is correct: `10.21203/rs.3.rs-9513298/v1`
- [ ] The novel analysis described matches what is actually in the manuscript.
- [ ] The disclosure is in a section the editor will actually read (cover
      letter, footnote on first page, or acknowledgments — per venue policy).
- [ ] Prior IEEE / Frontiers publications by the author (P1, P2, P3) are
      listed when the venue requests a publication record.
- [ ] No co-authors are named (single-author submission).
- [ ] Author's ORCID `0009-0009-7124-6669` is included.
